Hoisin Salmon Stir Fry

Description

A great easy and quick meal for when you are in a rush. Serves 2 people.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • 1 nest of dried noodles
  • 2 peppers
  • 1 courgette
  • canned beansprouts
  • 80g edamame beans
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp Hoisin Sauce
  • 1 tsp Light soy sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Out the noodles in s bowl and add boiling water to soften them.
  3. Put the salmon in an oven proof dish and coat with the hoisin sauce. Place in the oven for 15 mins.
  4. Whilst the salmon is cooking, in a wok, add a small drop of oil and put on a high heat. Add all of the vegetables to the pan and fry until they start to soften.
  5. Once the noodles are soft, drain the water from the bowl and add them to the pan. Make sure to turn the heat down to a low-medium heat to stop them from sticking to the pan.
  6. Remove the salmon from the oven and plate the stri-fry noodles, vegetables and salmon.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
