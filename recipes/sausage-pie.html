Sausage Pie

Description

Amazing comfort food for a person of any age. Easily modified and adptable for any taste and budget. Serves 2-4 people.

Ingredients

  • 6 good sized sausages
  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • 2 baking potatoes
  • 50ml skimmed milk
  • 15g of butter
  • 80g of grated cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Cut each sausage into approxately 3 or 4 chunks.
  3. Cover the bootom of a deep dish with the sausage chunks and put them in the oven to cook for approximately 20 mins.
  4. Whilst the sausages are cooking, peel the potatoes, chop them into small chunks and boil until soft.
  5. Once the potatoes are soft, add the milk and butter and then mash until they are the desired mash consistentcy.
  6. Take the sausages out of the oven and layer baked benas on top of the sausages.
  7. Add a layer of mash on top of the beans and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
  8. Return the layered ingredients to the oven until the cheese has melted and browned. The sauce from the baked beans may also be bubbling up the sides.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
